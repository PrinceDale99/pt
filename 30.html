The Importance of ICT in our daily life:
Information and Communication Technology (ICT) has become an integral part of our lives. It plays a crucial role in various aspects, including communication, education, business, and entertainment. ICT has transformed the way we connect with others, access information, and conduct everyday tasks. From smartphones to computers, it has made our lives more efficient and connected. In today's digital age, ICT is essential for staying informed, productive, and engaged in a rapidly changing world.
