Grilled Cheese Sandwiches with Bacon and Tomato

Ingredients:

  • 4 slices of bacon
  • 8 slices of bread
  • 4 tablespoons of butter
  • 8 slices of cheddar cheese
  • 2 ripe tomatoes, sliced

Instructions:

Instructions:
  1. Fry the bacon in a pan until crispy. Remove and drain on paper towels.
  2. Spread butter on one side of each slice of bread.
  3. Place a slice of cheddar cheese on the unbuttered side of four bread slices.
  4. Top with slices of bacon, tomato, and another slice of cheddar cheese.
  5. Top with the remaining bread slices, buttered side up.
  6. Heat a skillet or griddle over medium heat and place the sandwiches in the pan.
  7. Cook until the bread is golden brown and the cheese is melted, about 3-4 minutes per side.
  8. Remove from the pan, cut in half, and serve.
